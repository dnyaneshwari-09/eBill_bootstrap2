FAQs - eBill Application

The PDF copy generated on the website after online payment is valid as a payment receipt.

The receipt provided by any franchisee network agencies is authenticated as an MSEDCL payment receipt, and SMS is sent from MSEDCL to acknowledge the receipt. Further, the same is also updated at the MSEDCL website in payment history.

The online payment can be done at any time.

Part payment of energy bills is available on the website.

Online advance payment can be made. Just enter the amount you want to pay as advance instead of the bill amount.

The online payment can be done after the due date.

The additional security deposit amount can be paid online.

If the transaction is successfully received, the receipt will be updated within 3 working days from the date of transaction in Online Payment Option. If the transaction is a failure, the amount will be returned to the consumer's bank account.
